About us

SunBeam Cars. is a short or long term car rental service that aims to provide quality services to anyone looking for a rental car. The company SunbeamCars Inc. was established more than 30 years ago and today it rents out cars and among their customers you will find business people, tourists and people who need to rent a car because they do not have one. The company is located at a transport hub, from where buses and trains arrive and depart regularly.
